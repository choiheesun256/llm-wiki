# Polθ Inhibitors and BRCA Synthetic Lethality

## Summary
Nat Comms 2021 discovery of ART558, a potent Polθ (POLQ) polymerase inhibitor that elicits BRCA-gene synthetic lethality and targets PARPi resistance. ART558 inhibits theta-mediated end joining (TMEJ), synergizes with PARP inhibitors, and is effective against 53BP1/Shieldin-deficient cells that cause PARPi resistance. Provides a new drug class for BRCA-deficient cancers and PARPi-resistant tumors.

## Key points
- ART558 is first potent Polθ inhibitor
- Synthetic lethal with BRCA1/2 mutations
- Targets 53BP1/Shieldin-mediated PARPi resistance
- Synergizes with PARP inhibitors

## Project relevance
- Novel drug target for PARPi resistance
- Synthetic lethality beyond PARP
- DNA repair pathway targeting

## Caveats
- Preclinical compound
- In vitro/cell line focus
- Clinical development ongoing (novobiocin class)
