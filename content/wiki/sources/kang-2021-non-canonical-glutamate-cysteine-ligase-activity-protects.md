# Ferroptosis Regulators: 1,833 Compound Kinetic Profiling

> **Note**: PDF filename is "kang-2021-non-canonical-glutamate-cysteine-ligase-activity-protects" but actual content is Conlon et al. ferroptosis modulatory profiles paper.

## Summary

Large-scale screen of 1,833 small molecules (FDA-approved + investigational) using live-cell time-lapse imaging to build kinetic cell death modulatory profiles for apoptosis and ferroptosis. Identifies dozens of ferroptosis inhibitors with unexpected off-target antioxidant or iron chelating activities. ATP-competitive mTOR inhibitors (rapamycin analogs) are on-target ferroptosis inhibitors. Links amino acid sensing through mTOR-dependent and independent mechanisms to ferroptosis regulation.

## Key points

- 1,833 compounds profiled for apoptosis and ferroptosis modulation
- FDA-approved drugs with unexpected ferroptosis inhibitory activities discovered
- mTOR inhibitors are on-target ferroptosis inhibitors
- Amino acid sensing links mTOR to ferroptosis sensitivity

## Project relevance

- Drug repurposing: FDA-approved drugs with novel ferroptosis activities
- Compound pleiotropy revealing unexpected cell death pathway effects
- mTOR-ferroptosis axis as therapeutic target

## Caveats

- Preprint (bioRxiv); peer review status unclear
- In vitro screening; in vivo translation needed
- Cell line-specific; generalization across cancer types required
