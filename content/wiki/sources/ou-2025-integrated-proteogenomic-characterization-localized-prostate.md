# Localized Prostate Cancer Proteogenomics

## Summary

Integrated proteogenomic characterization of 145 localized prostate cancer patients from a Chinese cohort using WGS, RNA-seq, proteomics, and phosphoproteomics. Defines molecular subtypes with distinct AR signaling heterogeneity and therapeutic vulnerabilities. Provides East Asian population-specific PCa molecular landscape and treatment stratification framework.

## Key points

- 145-patient Chinese localized PCa proteogenomics
- Molecular subtypes with distinct AR signaling patterns
- Population-specific genomic/proteomic features
- Subtype-specific therapeutic vulnerabilities
- Treatment stratification framework

## Project relevance

- Large-scale proteogenomics study design
- Cancer molecular subtyping methodology
- Population-specific cancer characterization

## Caveats

- Chinese cohort; may differ from Western populations
- Localized PCa only; excludes advanced/metastatic
- Retrospective design; prospective validation needed
