# GBM Proteogenomic Characterization (Song 2017)

## Summary

Proteogenomic analysis of 8 GBM patients and matched normal brain tissue using label-free proteomics integrated with WGS and RNA-seq. Establishes genomic-proteomic correlations in glioblastoma as an early application of proteogenomics in brain tumors.

## Key points

- Early application of proteogenomics to GBM (n=8 cohort)
- Label-free shotgun proteomics integrated with WGS/RNA-seq
- GBM-specific protein alterations identified
- Genomic-proteomic correlation established

## Project relevance

- Reference for early proteogenomics methodology in brain tumors
- Label-free quantification approach as a baseline prior to TMT/DIA era
- Demonstrates feasibility of multi-omic integration in small tumor cohorts

## Caveats

- Very small cohort (n=8); limited statistical power
- 2017-era technology; restricted compared to current TMT/DIA approaches
- No independent validation cohort
