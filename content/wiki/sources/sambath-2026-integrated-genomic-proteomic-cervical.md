# Cervical Cancer Chemoradiation Resistance

## Summary

Integrated WGS and proteomics analysis of cervical cancer chemoradiation resistance. Compares resistant versus sensitive tumors to identify resistance-associated genomic alterations and proteomic signatures. Discovers biomarkers for treatment response prediction and potential therapeutic targets to overcome resistance.

## Key points

- Resistance-associated genomic alterations identified
- Proteomic signatures distinguish resistant/sensitive tumors
- Integrated biomarkers predict treatment response
- Therapeutic targets for overcoming resistance

## Project relevance

- Treatment resistance mechanism discovery
- Genomic-proteomic integration methodology
- Biomarker-guided therapy for cervical cancer

## Caveats

- Single-center cohort
- Retrospective study; prospective validation needed
- Limited functional validation
