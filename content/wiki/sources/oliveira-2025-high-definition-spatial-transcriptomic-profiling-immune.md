# Visium HD Immune Profiling in CRC

## Summary

Applies Visium HD high-definition spatial transcriptomics to profile immune cell populations in colorectal cancer at single-cell resolution (2μm bins). Maps the detailed immune landscape of the CRC tumor microenvironment, identifies immune cell niches and spatial neighborhoods, and correlates spatial immune organization with clinical features.

## Key points

- Visium HD achieves single-cell resolution in CRC tissue
- Detailed spatial mapping of immune cell populations
- Immune cell niches and neighborhoods identified
- Spatial organization correlates with clinical features
- Technology validated for immune profiling

## Project relevance

- High-definition spatial transcriptomics methodology
- Immune TME spatial analysis approach
- Visium HD technology benchmark

## Caveats

- CRC-specific; generalization needed
- High cost of Visium HD
- Limited cohort size
