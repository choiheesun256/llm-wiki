# ST + scRNA-seq Integration in PDAC

## Summary

Early framework for integrating microarray-based spatial transcriptomics with scRNA-seq in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma. Introduces multimodal intersection analysis (MIA) to annotate cell types in spatial regions. Reveals spatially restricted enrichment of ductal cells, macrophages, DCs, and cancer cell subpopulations, with colocalization of inflammatory fibroblasts and stress-response cancer cells.

## Key points

- Multimodal intersection analysis (MIA) for ST-scRNA-seq integration
- Spatially restricted cell-type enrichment in PDAC
- Inflammatory fibroblast-cancer cell colocalization
- Pioneer work in ST + single-cell integration

## Project relevance

- Foundational spatial transcriptomics integration method
- PDAC tumor microenvironment spatial architecture
- Reference for subsequent deconvolution methods

## Caveats

- Only 2 PDAC tumors analyzed
- Early-generation ST technology (lower resolution)
- Simple enrichment test; less sophisticated than deep learning methods
