# Human-centric Drug Development with NAMs

## Summary
Science 2026 perspective on New Approach Methodologies (NAMs) for human-centric drug development. Reviews organoids, organ-on-chip, computational models, and AI/ML approaches replacing animal testing. Discusses FDA Modernization Act 2.0, regulatory acceptance, and integration strategies for NAMs in preclinical development pipelines.

## Key points
- NAMs replacing animal models in preclinical drug development
- Organoids + organ-on-chip + AI integration as key direction
- FDA Modernization Act 2.0 provides regulatory foundation for NAM adoption
- Regulatory pathway for NAM validation and acceptance outlined

## Project relevance
- Drug development methodology relevant to repurposing validation
- Organoid/computational model applications for drug testing
- Regulatory landscape supporting in silico predictions

## Caveats
- Perspective/review with no original data
- Early-stage regulatory adoption with limited precedent
- Validation challenges for NAMs remain significant
