# NPC Radioresistance via scRNA-seq and Spatial Transcriptomics

## Summary
Nature Genetics 2025 study using scRNA-seq and spatial transcriptomics of 39 tumors from 24 NPC patients to compare primary and recurrent tumors. MCAM+ myofibroblastic CAFs promote radioresistance via collagen IV-ITGA2-FAK-AKT axis. Collagen IV suppresses T cell infiltration. CD8 ZNF683 cells in recurrent NPC show lower cytotoxicity.

## Key points
- MCAM+ CAFs drive radioresistance via collagen IV-FAK-AKT signaling
- Collagen IV suppresses T cell infiltration
- CD8 ZNF683 dysfunction in recurrent NPC
- CD47-SIRPα myeloid-tumor interaction mediates immune escape

## Project relevance
- ST + scRNA-seq integration methodology
- Radioresistance mechanisms in TME
- TME remodeling in recurrence

## Caveats
- NPC-specific findings
- 24 patients (moderate cohort size)
- Chinese cohort
