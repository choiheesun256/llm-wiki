# Schwann Cells in PDAC Microenvironment

## Summary
Nat Comms 2023 study using scRNA-seq and microarray-based spatial transcriptomics to reveal how Schwann cells drive malignant transformation in PDAC. Schwann cells push tumor cells toward basal-like subtype via Midkine signaling and CAFs toward inflammatory phenotype (iCAF) via IL-1α. Neuropathy correlates with worse prognosis.

## Key points
- Schwann cells drive basal-like tumor phenotype and iCAF conversion
- Midkine signaling mediates Schwann cell effects on tumor cells
- IL-1α switches CAFs to inflammatory subtype
- scRNA-seq + spatial transcriptomics characterize neuro-stroma niche in PDAC

## Project relevance
- Neural-tumor microenvironment interactions as therapeutic vulnerability
- Spatial transcriptomics methodology for TME characterization
- PDAC-specific therapeutic target identification

## Caveats
- Small spatial transcriptomics cohort
- Microarray-based ST (not sequencing-based, lower resolution)
- Limited to PDAC context
