# DL Prediction of Bladder Cancer Molecular Subtypes from H&E

## Summary

Deep learning model predicting BCG Response Subtypes (BRS) from H&E whole slide images in non-muscle invasive bladder cancer (NMIBC). Trained on 231 patients, achieves AUC 0.79 (internal) and 0.71 (external validation). Offers cost-effective alternative to molecular testing for predicting BCG treatment response.

## Key points

- H&E-based DL predicts BRS molecular subtypes (AUC 0.79/0.71)
- BRS correlates with BCG immunotherapy response
- Cost-effective alternative to molecular subtyping
- MIL framework on whole slide images

## Project relevance

- Deep learning for molecular subtype prediction from histology
- Treatment response biomarker from routine pathology
- Clinical decision support for bladder cancer therapy

## Caveats

- medRxiv preprint; not peer-reviewed
- Moderate external AUC (0.71) may limit clinical utility
- Small cohort (231 patients)
- Single-institution training data
