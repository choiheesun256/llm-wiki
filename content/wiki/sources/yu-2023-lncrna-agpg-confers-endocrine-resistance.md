# LncRNA AGPG and Endocrine Resistance in Breast Cancer

## Summary
Cancer Research 2023 study identifying lncRNA AGPG as a driver of endocrine resistance in ERα+ breast cancer. AGPG physically interacts with PURα, releasing E2F1 and activating E2F1 signaling. Epigenomically upregulated in resistant cells, especially luminal B subtype. Co-administration of AGPG siRNA and tamoxifen suppresses resistant tumor growth in xenografts.

## Key points
- lncRNA AGPG activates E2F1 by sequestering PURα
- Epigenomic activation in endocrine-resistant cells
- AGPG knockdown re-sensitizes to tamoxifen and CDK4/6i
- Luminal B subtype enrichment

## Project relevance
- Non-coding RNA in drug resistance mechanisms
- Endocrine therapy resistance molecular mechanism
- Potential therapeutic target identification

## Caveats
- Breast cancer specific
- In vitro/xenograft models only
- AGPG targeting challenging clinically
