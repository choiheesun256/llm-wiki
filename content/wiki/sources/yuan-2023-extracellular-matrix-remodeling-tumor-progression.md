# ECM Remodeling in Tumor Progression and Immune Escape

## Summary
Molecular Cancer 2023 comprehensive review of ECM remodeling in tumor progression and immune escape. Covers ECM composition, remodeling enzymes (MMPs, LOX), mechanical signaling, and impacts on proliferation, metastasis, angiogenesis, and immune suppression. Discusses ECM normalization as therapeutic strategy.

## Key points
- ECM composition and remodeling mechanisms in cancer
- Mechanical properties (stiffness) regulate tumor behavior
- ECM creates immunosuppressive microenvironment
- ECM normalization as therapeutic concept
