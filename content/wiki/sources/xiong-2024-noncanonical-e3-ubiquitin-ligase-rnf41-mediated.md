# RNF41-MYO1C Ubiquitination in Prostate Cancer Metastasis

## Summary
Oncogene 2024 study identifying RNF41 as a noncanonical E3 ubiquitin ligase that ubiquitinates MYO1C to promote prostate cancer metastasis. RNF41-mediated K63-linked ubiquitination of MYO1C enhances cell migration and invasion through cytoskeletal remodeling. RNF41 overexpression correlates with poor prognosis and metastatic progression.

## Key points
- RNF41 ubiquitinates MYO1C via K63 linkage (non-degradative)
- Promotes prostate cancer metastasis through cytoskeletal remodeling
- RNF41 overexpression correlates with poor prognosis
- RNF41 proposed as prognostic marker and therapeutic target

## Project relevance
- Ubiquitin signaling mechanisms in cancer metastasis
- E3 ligase as potential therapeutic target
- Network module for metastasis-related pathway analysis

## Caveats
- Single cancer type (prostate cancer)
- Mechanistic study without clinical validation
- Relies on in vitro and xenograft models
