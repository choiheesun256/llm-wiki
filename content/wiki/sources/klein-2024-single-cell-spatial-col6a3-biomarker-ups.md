# COL6A3 as Prognostic Biomarker in UPS

## Summary

Single-cell and Visium spatial transcriptomics of undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma (UPS) vs. atypical fibroxanthoma (AFX) identifies COL6A3 as a prognostic biomarker. High COL6A3 expression in UPS associates with poor prognosis. Spatial mapping confirms stromal localization. Validated by IHC and survival analysis.

## Key points

- COL6A3 identified as UPS prognostic biomarker via scRNA-seq
- High COL6A3 → poor prognosis in UPS
- Spatial transcriptomics confirms stromal localization
- Differentiates UPS from AFX

## Project relevance

- Single-cell biomarker discovery methodology
- Spatial validation of transcriptomic findings
- Rare tumor characterization approach

## Caveats

- Rare tumor type limits sample size and statistical power
- Single biomarker (not multi-marker panel)
- Retrospective; needs prospective validation
- Functional role of COL6A3 in UPS not established
