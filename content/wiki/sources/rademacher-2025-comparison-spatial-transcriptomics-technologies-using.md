# Spatial Transcriptomics Technology Comparison

## Summary

Systematic comparison of four imaging-based spatial transcriptomics methods (RNAscope HiPlex, Molecular Cartography, Merscope, Xenium) alongside sequencing-based Visium, using medulloblastoma MBEN fresh frozen cryosections. Establishes QC framework for evaluating sensitivity, specificity, and cell segmentation quality. Demonstrates that imaging-based methods capture intricate tumor microanatomy and that reimaging can improve accuracy.

## Key points

- 5 ST platforms benchmarked on same tumor tissue
- Imaging-based methods capture MBEN microanatomy well
- Sensitivity and specificity vary across platforms
- Cell segmentation quality differs significantly
- Technology selection framework established

## Project relevance

- ST technology selection guide
- QC metrics for spatial transcriptomics
- Benchmark methodology for platform comparison

## Caveats

- Single tissue type (MBEN) tested
- Fresh frozen only; no FFPE comparison
- Rapidly evolving field; new platforms not covered
