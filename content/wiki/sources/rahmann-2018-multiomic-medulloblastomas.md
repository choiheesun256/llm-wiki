# Multiomic Medulloblastomas (Preview)

## Summary

Cancer Cell Preview discussing medulloblastoma proteomics studies (Archer et al. and Forget et al., 2018). Highlights how proteomics reveals subclasses beyond epigenomic/transcriptomic classification, including ERBB4-NRG-SRC signaling as a hallmark of Group 4 and MYC post-translational modifications with PRKDC dependency in Group 3 medulloblastoma.

## Key points

- Proteomics reveals subclasses beyond transcriptomic subtypes
- ERBB4-NRG-SRC signaling hallmark of Group 4
- MYC PTMs and PRKDC dependency in Group 3
- Multi-omics essential for complete tumor characterization
