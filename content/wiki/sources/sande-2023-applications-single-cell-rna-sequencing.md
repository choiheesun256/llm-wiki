# scRNA-seq in Drug Discovery

## Summary

Nature Reviews Drug Discovery review of scRNA-seq applications across the drug discovery pipeline: target identification, mechanism of action elucidation, toxicology assessment, and patient stratification. Covers integration with perturbation screens (Perturb-seq) and practical considerations for pharmaceutical industry adoption.

## Key points

- scRNA-seq enables cell type-specific target discovery
- Drug MOA elucidated at single-cell resolution
- Toxicology improved by cellular heterogeneity analysis
- Integration with perturbation screens (Perturb-seq)

## Project relevance

- Drug discovery pipeline methodology review
- Single-cell applications in pharmacology
- Perturbation screen technology overview

## Caveats

- Review; no new experimental data
- Published 2023; spatial/multi-modal integration rapidly evolving
- Cost/scalability barriers for industry adoption
